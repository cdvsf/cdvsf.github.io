About Me

The Designer
Behind the Screen

2nd
Year UX Design

Hey — I'm Kanaad Raut. A second-year UX designer who believes the best interfaces are the ones people don't have to think about. I'm based in Nagpur, India, and obsessed with the intersection of human behavior, visual design, and frontend craft.

My approach starts with deep user research — understanding not just what people do, but why they do it. From there I move through wireframing, rapid prototyping, and usability testing until the experience clicks. Then I bring it to life with code.

I'm currently studying design full-time while building real-world projects that push the boundaries of what UX can feel like — not just functional, but memorable.
